Getting SLOs in place is only the first step. We’re introducing Nobl9 SLO Oversight to help teams keep them accurate, relevant, and useful over time. It’s wild to think that we started Nobl9 over six years ago. You learn a lot in six years. When we started this journey into the world of modern reliability engineering, we were guided by the principle that reliability work is mission-critical. Yet so often, it’s relegated to an afterthought – and measured in ridiculous ways like counting incidents.
